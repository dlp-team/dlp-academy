# Subplan 06 - Role Management Audit and Switching

## Scope
- Audit whether institution admins should be able to also act as teachers.
- Implement robust same-identity dual-role switching if approved.

## Proposed Direction
- Allow dual-role at identity level with explicit active-role switcher.
- Keep authorization checks tied to assigned roles and active context.

## Acceptance Criteria
- No account duplication requirement for same email.
- Users can switch view context without logout.
- Security checks still evaluate assigned capabilities correctly.
